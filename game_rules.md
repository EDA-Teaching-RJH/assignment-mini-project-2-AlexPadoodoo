Game Name: Farkle
(A dice game where you score points depending on the dice you role)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Rules of the game:
- You have 6, 6 sided dice
- Rolling different numbers scores different amount of points
- Rolling a 1 scores 100 points
- Rolling a 5 scores 50 points 
- Rolling 3 of a kind is worth 100 points multiplied by the number on the dice
    (e.g. Rolling 3, 4 sided dice is 400 points. 3 1s is 1000 points)
- 4 or more of a kind is worth double the points of 3 of a kind
    (e.g. Rolling 4 4s is 800 points and 5 4s is 1600 points)
- The aim of the game is to be the first person to score atleast 2000 points

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
How to play the game:
- The player starts by rolling 6 dice and then sets aside any scoring die - these go towards the total score
- The player can then choose to roll the remaining dice again, or score their points and pass their go to the opponent. (The opponent will then do the same)
- If no scoring dice are rolled, then the player loses their current score and it becomes the opponents turn.
- If the player scores all 6 dice, then they can roll again.
